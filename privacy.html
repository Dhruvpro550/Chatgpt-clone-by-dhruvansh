Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Our top priority is your privacy! This web tool does not track your private chat files or store text messages on our servers.

At Simple AI Chatbot, accessible from our main portal, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by our site and how we use it.

1. Log Files and Analytical Data

Like most standard websites, we follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit web pages. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/assigned exit pages, and number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends and administering the site.

2. Google Cookies and Third-Party Ads

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to various pages across the internet. Visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy guidelines. Some advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons to check promotional click rates automatically.

3. Children's Privacy Protection (COPPA)

Another core part of our mission is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and monitor their online activity. Our interactive application is completely safe for children age 5 and above. We do not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children. If you think your child provided this kind of information on our website, please contact us immediately.

4. Cookies Policy

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, understand website traffic, and provide better services. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but some website features may not work properly.

5. Google AdSense and Advertising

We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google and its partners may use cookies to show ads based on a user's previous visits to this website or other websites. Users may manage their advertising preferences through Google's advertising settings.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through our Contact page available on this website.
